Solar-Powered Phone Charger

Materials Required:

  • Solar panel
  • USB charging circuit
  • Rechargeable battery
  • Enclosure
  • Wires and connectors

Procedure:

  1. Connect the solar panel to the USB charging circuit.
  2. Connect the rechargeable battery to the USB charging circuit.
  3. Enclose the components in the enclosure.
  4. Connect the USB output to your phone for charging.
